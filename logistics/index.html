PROBLEM

Global logistics networks rely on accurate information, yet most checkpoints validate documentation - not the actual goods being moved.

Mislabeling, misrouting, contamination, counterfeits, incorrect batching, missing chain-of-custody data, and unverifiable environmental conditions create massive operational failures. Packages, pallets, containers, and materials often move through the world with assumptions instead of verified truth.

When a shipment is wrong, late, damaged, mislabeled, or fraudulent, the entire downstream operation pays the price.

BREAKDOWN

Logistics workflows depend on scanning labels, barcodes, manifests, and digital entries - none of which confirm that the physical goods match the documented information.

A shipment may claim a specific product but contain something different. A container may pass through multiple checkpoints without ever being physically validated. Environmental-controlled products may drift out of tolerance. Counterfeit goods enter legitimate corridors. Chain-of-custody records break down during transfers.

The system moves fast, but verification remains slow, incomplete, or nonexistent.

CLARITY SOLUTION

ALETHRA™ introduces real-time verification throughout the global supply chain.

Using multi-sensor intelligence, material fingerprinting, and environmental normalization, ALETHRA™ verifies that goods, materials, and containers are exactly what they claim to be - at every handoff, checkpoint, and transit point. It confirms authenticity, integrity, condition, and compliance without relying solely on labels or documents.

Supporting Statements:

  • Confirms product identity at every scan point
  • Detects mislabeling, fraud, or substitution
  • Monitors temperature, humidity, and environmental drift
  • Validates packaging and seal integrity
  • Protects chain-of-custody from origin to final destination
  • Eliminates blind spots across multimodal transport
  • Provides regulators and partners with verified truth data

ALETHRA™ transforms global logistics by providing clarity where the world needs it most - across movement.
