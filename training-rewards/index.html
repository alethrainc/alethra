DESIGN RULES (LOCKED)

  • • Rewards are earned, not gamed
  • • Quality matters more than volume
  • • Verification matters more than opinion

  • • No apparel includes third-party logos
  • • Physical rewards are finite and meaningful

CORE MECHANISM:

CLARITY CREDITS (CC)

Clarity Credits are an internal system used to determine contributor tier eligibility.

  • • Credits are not public
  • • Credits are earned only for accepted, reviewed, or verified contributions
  • • Credits are weighted by long-term usefulness and impact
  • • Credits unlock tiers, not individual items
