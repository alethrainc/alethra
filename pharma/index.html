PROBLEM

Pharmaceutical and life science environments run on precision, yet they are vulnerable to errors caused by unverified inputs.

Raw materials, active ingredients, reagents, cell lines, samples, labels, formulations, and environmental conditions all carry the risk of being incorrect, contaminated, or mislabeled. Even small discrepancies can invalidate batches, compromise research, delay trials, trigger recalls, or jeopardize patient safety.

Without verified truth, the entire development and manufacturing pipeline remains exposed.

BREAKDOWN

Pharma workflows often rely on documentation, supplier certifications, manual checks, and digital records that do not confirm the actual identity or integrity of materials.

A reagent may match its label but be compromised. An active ingredient may be substituted or diluted. A sample may be mismatched to the wrong patient or study group. Environmental controls may drift out of safe tolerance without detection. Traditional scanners and inspection tools cannot validate molecular integrity, detect contamination, or confirm authenticity.

Breakdowns occur silently - and escalate quickly.

CLARITY SOLUTION

ALETHRA™ introduces real-time verification across pharmaceutical development, manufacturing, and research workflows.

Using multi-sensor intelligence, material fingerprinting, environmental normalization, and truth scoring, ALETHRA™ validates the identity, purity, and integrity of raw materials, reagents, finished products, environmental conditions, and sample records. It confirms what labels, barcodes, and COAs cannot, providing regulatory-grade truth at every step.
