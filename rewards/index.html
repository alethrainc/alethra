PURPOSE

This page explains how ALETHRA™ recognizes contributors who help train ALETHRA™ and ALETHA™ through verified, responsible participation.

Recognition is not based on volume, speed, or opinion.

It is based on clarity, verification, and real-world impact.

HOW RECOGNITION WORKS

ALETHRA™ uses an internal evaluation system called Clarity Credits to assess contributions over time.

Clarity Credits:

  • Are internal and not visible to the public
  • Cannot be traded, transferred, or requested
  • Are earned only after contributions are reviewed or verified
  • Reflect long-term usefulness rather than activity volume

Clarity Credits determine eligibility for contributor recognition tiers.

CONTRIBUTOR RECOGNITION TIERS

This tier recognizes approved participants who have made at least one reviewed contribution.

Recognition includes:

  • Contributor designation displayed in the application
  • Early access to selected application features
  • Advanced explanation mode for supported responses
  • Ability to flag uncertainty for review
  • ALETHRA™ private-label ceramic coffee mug
  • ALETHRA™ sticker pack

VERIFIED TRAINER

This tier recognizes contributors whose work consistently meets ALETHRA™ verification and clarity standards.

Recognition includes:

  • Verified Trainer designation displayed in the application
  • Permanent access to advanced reasoning views
  • Confidence and uncertainty indicators
  • Priority access to new ALETHRA™ capabilities
  • Invitations to private product and training briefings
  • ALETHRA™ private-label premium hoodie
  • ALETHRA™ private-label hat or beanie
  • ALETHRA™ private-label premium T-shirt

SIGNAL LEADER

This tier recognizes sustained, high-impact contributors whose work continues to provide value over time.

Recognition includes:

  • Signal Leader designation displayed in the application
  • Permanent unlocking of all application upgrades
  • Access to contributor-only analytics
  • Participation in closed training reviews
  • Limited-run ALETHRA™ private-label premium apparel
  • ALETHRA™ branded YETI® Rambler tumbler
  • ALETHRA™ branded YETI® travel mug
  • ALETHRA™ branded YETI® soft cooler
  • Numbered ALETHRA™ contributor card

ENTERPRISE SIGNAL PARTNER

This tier recognizes enterprise-grade contributors and institutional partners.

Recognition includes:

  • Enterprise Signal Partner designation
  • Direct feedback and escalation channels with ALETHRA™ leadership
  • Invitations to private roadmap and validation sessions
  • Priority access to enterprise features and integrations
  • Custom ALETHRA™ private-label premium apparel
  • Full ALETHRA™ branded YETI® enterprise gear set
  • Optional advisory or paid engagement pathways

WHAT ALETHRA™ DOES NOT REWARD

To protect clarity and trust, ALETHRA™ does not reward:

  • Speed or volume of submissions
  • Opinions without verification
  • Popularity, voting, or public rankings
  • Competition between contributors
  • Guessing or speculative answers

TRANSPARENCY AND SAFEGUARDS

Participation in Crowd Training does not guarantee recognition, rewards, or tier advancement.

ALETHRA™ reserves the right to:

  • Review contributions at any time
  • Adjust recognition tiers
  • Pause or revoke contributor access
  • Decline fulfillment if standards are not met

All recognition decisions prioritize safety, accuracy, and responsibility.

ALETHRA™ is building a global standard for verified truth in real-world systems.

Contributor recognition reflects trust, responsibility, and meaningful impact.
