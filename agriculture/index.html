PROBLEM

Agriculture suffers from one of the most damaging global verification problems: uncertainty. Farmers, suppliers, processors, and regulators all rely on labels, disclosures, and assumptions that often fail to reflect reality.

Pesticide contamination, misclassified products, incorrect origin claims, feed inconsistencies, soil inaccuracies, and fraudulent certifications cost the world billions and undermine trust across the entire food system.

Without verified truth, contamination spreads, recalls scale, and consumers are left unprotected.

BREAKDOWN

Agricultural workflows depend on manual checks, supplier declarations, and paper-based certifications that do not verify what actually exists on the product or in the environment.

A crop may be labeled organic yet contain pesticide residue. A shipment may claim a specific origin but come from a different region. A feed supply may not match its stated formulation. Soil conditions may be misreported or outdated.

Current tools cannot detect real-world discrepancies fast enough to prevent contamination or fraud.

The system depends on trust, not truth - and that is where breakdowns occur.

CLARITY SOLUTION

ALETHRA™ brings real-time, field-to-market verification to agriculture using multi-sensor intelligence, material fingerprinting, and pesticide detection algorithms.

It confirms the identity, integrity, and environmental condition of crops, products, feed, soil, water, and agricultural materials at the exact point where risk enters the supply chain.

ALETHRA™ validates what labels cannot and certifies what current systems only assume.

Supporting Statements:

  • Detects pesticide residues on crops and products
  • Confirms organic, free-range, and sustainable claims
  • Verifies soil and water quality conditions
  • Identifies fraudulent or mislabeled agricultural goods
  • Protects processors and distributors from contaminated inputs
  • Creates verified transparency across the entire farm-to-table chain

OUTCOME

Agricultural operations become safer, cleaner, and more profitable. Pesticide confusion disappears. Organic and sustainability claims become verifiable. Farmers gain transparency. Supply chains gain certainty. Regulators receive real-time clarity. Processors avoid contaminated inputs. Consumers receive products they can trust.

The outcome is a global agricultural system with integrity at every step - from soil to shelf.
